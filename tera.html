Summary

Home Chef and Nutritionist in your Pocket.

  • Calorie is a home chef and nutritionist in your pocket. The app helps deliver to you health food based on your diet and health requirements on a subscription model.

    • Deliverables

  • Problem Solution Hypothesis.
  • User Research.
  • Wire Framing.
  • Visual Design.
  • Branding.

    • Problem

  • 1. Cooking healthy and tasty food that cater to customer lifestyle
  • 2. Cost to stay healthy is high.
  • 3. Socialising on a diet.
  • 4. Cravings on a diet.

    • Solution

  • 1. Reducing Choices to be made on Dietary food.
  • 2. Save Money, Eating Healthy.
  • 3. Home Delivered Meals.
  • 4. Socialise feature to help user make healthier choice eating out.

    • User Research

    Statistics

  • 400 users were spoken to
  • 90% said they want to get healthy.
  • 85% said they had tried dieting and failed before.
  • 90% said they found it tough to socialise on a diet.
  • 98% said they have cravings on a diet.
  • 72% said cooking diet food is hectic.
  • 65% said diet food gets monotonous.
  • 87% nutritionists are over priced.

    • Branding

    Logo Breakdown

  • While designing the logo I was on the look for something that could closely symbolise "calorie". What a calorie could look like? Calories are fuel to our body, calories to the body are substances it can burn to create energy. What I came up with was a fire, flame like looking logo.

    • Color Scheme

  • Trust is the building block in local businesses so this logo would be most relevant.Similarly the colour chosen is a Blue which symbolises trust between the customer and seller.
